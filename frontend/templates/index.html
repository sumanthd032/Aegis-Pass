About Aegis Pass

Aegis Pass is a powerful, secure password generator designed to create strong, random passwords and memorable passphrases to protect your online accounts.

Password Types

  • Random: Creates a classic, strong password using a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters. Ideal for password managers and maximum security.
  • Memorable: Creates a secure passphrase by joining random words (e.g., "correct-horse-battery-staple"). These are easier for humans to remember while still being very difficult to guess.

The strength meter estimates how difficult a password would be to crack. Always aim for "Strong" or "Very Strong" for your important accounts.
